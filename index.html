Intro

Thank you for visiting my profile. My name is Joaquín Sarmiento and I studied Social Sciences (History) at the University. Later I specialized in statistics and I.T. and I am currently researching the implementation of renewable energy systems from a political economy perspective.

I have participated in numerous research projects on renewable energy policies and their impact on rural habitat. I work as a professor and researcher at the Catholic University of Salta and as a teacher at the Ministry of Education of this Province.

I believe that it is necessary to increase the quality of the social sciences from a greater literacy in statistics and mathematics and the use of the potential of computational systems. This, naturally, does not go to the detriment of qualitative methodologies that must be just as rigorous.

I'm a big fan of free software philosophy, French literature, Italian microhistory, and New Zealand culture. You can check out some of my work here.

If you consider that my collaboration can add value, do not hesitate to contact me at jrsarmiento@ucasal.com.ar .
